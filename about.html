About Youwind

Accelerating Offshore Wind

At Youwind Renewables we aim at filling the gap towards the so needed green transtition. We strive to accelerate the deployment offshore wind technologies that enhance the skill set of those most eager to take on the challenge. By merging engineering, technology and finance we provide professionals with the optimal tools towards building a more sustainable future.

Our Team

Edvald Edvaldsson
CEO & Co-founder

Edvald is a Mechanical & Energy Engineer from the Royal Institute of Technology in Sweden. He has a bbroad experience from the offshore wind power industry from Orsted (Denmark) and from the financial industry (UK and Iceland). Edvald has worked as a lead consultants for both project developers and wind turbine manufacturers since 2017.
He is an expert in layout design and yield calculations, wind resource assesments, technical project developement and financial evaluation.
Edvald had the idea of creating the Youwind paltform after wondering why companies and consultants spend ages finding data and sorting out spaghetto spreadsheets and started the core development of the now commercial IT-solutions to provide disruptive time savings in project development.
